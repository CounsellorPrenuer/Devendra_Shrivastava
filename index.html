Air Vice Marshal Devendra Shrivastava (Veteran) is a Mentoria certified career counsellor who brings an exceptional blend of strategic leadership, people development and future readiness to the counselling space. With 36 years of distinguished service in the Indian Air Force as an aeronautical engineer, he has led large high stake teams, commanded four Air Force Units, headed the IAF's communication and IT vertical and overseen critical maintenance operations of aircrafts and helicopters in Eastern Air Command.

Today, he combines this vast experience with his Mentoria training to guide students and working professionals with clarity, confidence, and a deep sense of purpose. His counsel empowers young learners to make informed, future proof career decisions in an increasingly dynamic world.
